Rupandehi Lilaram Neupane Secondary School

Shree Rupandehi Lilaram Neupane Ma Vi. or RLNSS, previously known as Shree 5 Indra Rajya Laxmi Ma Vi., is located in the heart of Bhairahawa. Established in 2012 BS, it has been recognized as the best government school of Siddharthanagar municipality. With over 2200 students every academic session, RLNSS has produced professionals in various sectors such as Doctors, Engineers, Pilots, CAs, Bankers, Entrepreneurs, Industrialists, and Government employees in its 67 years of excellence.
